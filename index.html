Welcome to noresm2cmor's project page

noresm2cmor is a FORTRAN based command line tool for post-processing NorESM output using the Climate Model Output Rewriter libraries.

Support or Contact

Sponsors

The development of the tool has been funded by the Research Council of Norway through the projects Integrated Earth System Approach to Explore Natural Variability and Climate Sensitivity (EarthClim) and Earth system modelling of climate Variations in the Anthropocene (EVA).
